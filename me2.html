About Infosys

Infosys is a multinational corporation founded in India by Narayana Murthy, and six other co-founders in 1981. Headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Infosys is a global leader in technology consulting and services. It is service-based company.The company specializes in IT services, including software development, system integration, and business consulting. Infosys is renowned for its innovative solutions in areas like artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and digital transformation.
